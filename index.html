ABOUT

Cook Creatively is a service whose main task is to tell all concerned people about the rules of the culinary competition and in general about the whole event, the winners of past years. Also to provide ideas for your meals and a discount on tuition of culinary art, to attract new people to the turtropa and, in particular, to the culinary competition.
Our motto: Cook Cretively!
This means that we welcome your originality, soleness and we give you basis for the disclosure of yourself and your potential. We allow you to be yourself. Is not it important, to be yourself in our imperfect world?
May be exactly our event will give you a push to a new life and will help you to enlist with your vocation!
Our goal is to interest you and show all your best qualities upon condition that you will gather the most unique experiences that will remain forever in your memory.
Thank you for visiting us!
If we have interested you, then register your team for participation.
Let the strongest win!
Best regards from the team Cook Creatively!

RULES

Main Rules

Overview:
Turtropa will be held on the 10th of September. All non-resident who have bought tickets for the train immediately should surrender them. For the absence on the event first-year students should write an explanation to Mr. Kursov. You should not have problems with him. Gathering is on Saturday at 9:30 am at the station under the panel. Curator collects money for the train and buys tickets for all. Strongest request, do not be late!

Appearance:
This year the theme is "subculture". Each group has a certain topic and you must correspond it, it is estimated very well! For your costume the whole group can get bonuses!

Performance:
It is necessary to think of a creative sketch for 2 (two) minutes, no more. There will be a column for background music and four microphones. Creative sketch should correspond to the topic.

Heat:
Your team will participate in the race with pickets. The results of the race will be evaluated highly. Perhaps exactly your result will bring the win to your team!

Time:
The event will end at 4 (four) o'clock in the afternoon and you should take food with yourself. You can prepare a dish on fire with your team, it will be evaluated highly. Also the culinary competition is worth mentioning. You will be preparing dish for the jury. You will get one random ingredient which is need to be used. The jury will evaluate your dish on the criteria of taste, originality, beautiful description and appearance.

Pernicious habits:
You can take cigarettes with yourself, both electronic and conventional. Your curator is personally responsible for you and your well-being. You are not allowed to smoke on the territory but there will be allocated a place for smoking. Drinking alcohol and using of psychotropic substances is strictly prohibited!

Goal:
The goal of all events is creation of a team. You should remember this. Seniors of your specialty entrust on you great hopes and they will be upset if you don't show yourself from the best side. So, you should be a team. On turtropa chief over you is curator. And if he will be absent â€“ your captain. Groupmates can make some suggestions but you must not turn into what some not very cultured people call circus.
